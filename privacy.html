Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how MyBox Studios (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you access our website, mobile experience, or any services connected to MyBox Studios.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, and optional profile details.
  • Payment Information: Processed by Stripe. MyBox Studios never stores or sees your full credit card number.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, device type, and user activity.
  • Creator Content: Films, music, and media uploaded by creators.

How We Use Your Information

  • Process payments securely through Stripe
  • Provide access to purchased content
  • Manage creator accounts and studios
  • Improve performance, stability, and user experience
  • Provide customer support

Stripe Payments

Stripe handles all financial transactions. No payment processing is performed on MyBox Studios servers.

Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies to improve performance and personalize your browsing experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

Data Protection

We use industry-standard practices to safeguard your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Third-Party Services

MyBox Studios integrates with platforms such as Stripe, Netlify, hosting/CDN providers, and optional analytics tools. These services may collect additional information under their own policies.

Your Rights

  • Request the deletion of your data
  • Correct inaccuracies in your data
  • Opt out of non-essential emails
