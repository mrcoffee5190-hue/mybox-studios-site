Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how MyBox Studios (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you access our website, app, or any services connected to MyBox Studios.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and other optional profile details.

How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

Stripe Payments

All financial transactions are handled by Stripe. No payment information is processed directly by MyBox Studios.

We may use cookies or similar technologies to improve performance and personalize your experience. You may disable cookies in your browser if you prefer.
